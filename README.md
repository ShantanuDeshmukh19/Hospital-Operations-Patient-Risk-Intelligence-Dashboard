## Executive Overview

The **Hospital Operations & Patient Risk Intelligence Dashboard** is an advanced healthcare analytics solution designed to provide a comprehensive view of hospital performance, patient risk metrics, operational efficiency, and financial insights.

This dashboard helps healthcare executives, hospital administrators, operational managers, and analysts monitor key hospital metrics in real time and make data-driven decisions that improve patient care, optimize resource utilization, and increase operational efficiency.

The dashboard consolidates critical healthcare KPIs, trends, demographic analysis, financial metrics, and departmental performance into a single interactive interface.

---

# Dashboard Objectives

The primary objectives of this dashboard are:

- Monitor hospital operational performance
- Analyze patient admission and readmission trends
- Improve patient care quality
- Reduce readmission and mortality rates
- Optimize bed occupancy and resource allocation
- Track hospital financial performance
- Support strategic healthcare planning
- Enable executive-level decision-making

# Executive KPI Summary

The top section of the dashboard displays high-level healthcare KPIs.

## KPIs Included

| KPI | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Total Patients | Total number of patients treated |
| Total Admissions | Total patient admissions |
| Readmission Rate | Percentage of patients readmitted |

| Bed Occupancy | Percentage of occupied hospital beds |

Each KPI includes:

# KPI Calculations

## Readmission Rate

Readmission Rate = (Readmitted Patients / Total Discharged Patients) × 100

## Mortality Rate

Mortality Rate = (Number of Deaths / Total Patients) × 100

## Bed Occupancy Rate

Bed Occupancy Rate = (Occupied Beds / Total Available Beds) × 100

## Average Length of Stay

Average Length of Stay = Total Inpatient Days / Total Discharges
